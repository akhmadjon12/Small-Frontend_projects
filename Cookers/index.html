Our Dishes


“Cook” restaurant is an approachable, fun and introspective look at the process of being a restaurant. The 80-seat New American Bistro is located in the Greater-Boston area in Needham, Massachusetts. Cook presents a menu that crosses all cultural lines and spans from the simple to the complex. The perfect place to meet for dinner, enjoy craft cocktails and meet friends, Cook is a true neighborhood restaurant in every way. Executive Chef/Owner Chef Paul Turano is dedicated to using only the freshest local ingredients. At Cook the food is dressed up, but you can dress down.
